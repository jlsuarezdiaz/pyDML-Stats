Welcome to pyDML Stats webpage

In this webpage you can find several tables and graphics evaluating the performance of the algorithms in pyDML.

The experiments

We have developed the following 4 blocks of experiments:

  • Basic experiments. Evaluation of all the algorithms capable of learning at maximum dimension, with k-nearest neighbors classification.
  • Nearest centroid experiments. Evaluation of centroid-based distance learning algorithms with their corresponding centroid-based classifiers.
  • Kernel experiments. Evaluation of kernel-based algorithms, with k-nearest neighbors classification.
  • Dimensionality reduction experiments. Evaluation of dimensionality reduction algorithms, for different dimensions, with k-nearest neighbors classification.

Details

  • Evaluation metrics. The classification scores of k-nearest neighbors classifiers (k-NN) and nearest class mean (NCM) or nearest class with k centroids (NCM (k ctrd)) have been used.
  • Validation scheme. 10-fold cross validation.
  • Normalization. Min-max normalization.
  • Datasets. Up to 34 standard classification datasets from KEEL.
